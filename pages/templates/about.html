Welcome to our company

Welcome to Cool Pets, your number one source for all things pet related. We're dedicated to giving you the very best pet products, with a focus on dogs. Founded in 2000 by John Fendler, Cool Pets has come a long way from its beginnings in Port Townsend, Washington. When John first started out, his passion for pet products, drove him to quit his day job and do tons of research, so that Cool Pets can offer you quality products at competitive prices. We now serve customers all across the country and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into offering our pet supplies to you, on our website. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Sincerely, Cool Pets
